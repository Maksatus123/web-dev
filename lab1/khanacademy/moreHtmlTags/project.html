Spaghetti Bolognese

Jump to Recipe
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef 1 lb
Tomato sauce 2 cups

Details:

  • Preparation time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking time: 30 minutes

Steps:

  1. Cook ground beef in a large pan until browned.
  2. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  4. Serve Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Caprese Salad

Jump to Recipe
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 4 large
Mozzarella cheese 1 ball

Details:

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • No cooking required

Steps:

  1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.
  2. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and sprinkle with fresh basil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
